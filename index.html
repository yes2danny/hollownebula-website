Reefling

A Cozy Desktop Aquarium

A tiny, transparent aquarium that sits quietly in the corner of your screen while you work, browse, or game. Your fish wander, the water glows, and your desktop shows through the glass. Clicks outside the tank pass right through — company, never in the way.

🖥️

Lives on your desktop

A small, borderless, always-on-top window that parks in the corner of your screen. Transparent glass lets your wallpaper show through. The interface fades when you're not using it — just water and fish.

🐚

Feed, earn, collect

Click to drop food and watch fish swim over to eat. Earn shells slowly over time — a gentle idle currency, not a grind. Spend them in the Store to bring home new species from a roster of 161 — 134 pixel-art fish plus seafloor critters like snails, crabs, and starfish.

🌊

Slow discovery

New species surface as you play — a relaxed, weeks-long collection that rewards coming back each day, not marathon sessions. Impatient? Any fish can be brought home early for double its price.

🪸

Make it yours

Decorate with seaweed, kelp, coral, sea fans, rocks, and iridescent shells. Name every fish. A day/night cycle drifts the water from bright noon to deep blue night. The tank grows livelier as your community grows.

☮️

Designed to be peaceful

Fish never starve — idle for hours or days and your tank is always fine. Soft aquarium ambience, a daily shell gift with streak bonuses, and poke-to-scatter when you want a little interaction.

🏆

Steam features

Steam Achievements for milestones like your first fish, a full school, and discovering every species. Family Sharing supported. A calm companion built for the corner of your screen.

Reefling is made to be the calmest thing on your screen. Drop in, feed a fish, and get back to your day.
