Lives on your desktop

A small, borderless window that stays on top and parks in the corner of your screen. The glass is transparent, so your wallpaper shows through. When you're not using it the interface fades away and you're left with water and fish.

🐚

Feeding and collecting

Click to drop food and watch your fish swim over to eat. Shells build up slowly while you play, so the currency never turns into a grind. Spend them in the Store to bring home new species from a roster of 161: 134 pixel-art fish plus seafloor critters like snails, crabs, and starfish.

🌊

Slow discovery

New species surface as you play. The full collection takes weeks, and it's built for a few minutes a day rather than long marathon sessions. Impatient? Any fish can be brought home early for double its price.

🪸

Make it yours

Decorate with seaweed, kelp, coral, sea fans, rocks, and iridescent shells. Name every fish. A day/night cycle drifts the water from bright noon to deep blue night. The tank grows livelier as your community grows.

☮️

Peaceful by default

Your fish never starve. Leave the tank for hours or days and it's always fine when you come back. There's soft aquarium ambience, a daily shell gift that grows with your streak, and a poke-to-scatter when you feel like stirring things up.

🏆

Steam features

Steam Achievements for milestones like your first fish, a full school, and tracking down every species. Family Sharing is supported too.

It's there when you want a small break. Feed a fish or just watch the water for a minute, then get back to whatever you were doing.
