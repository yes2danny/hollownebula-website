Reefling: quick fixes

  • Game won't start? Update your graphics drivers. Reefling needs OpenGL 3.3 support.
  • Black or invisible window? Try reinstalling from Steam. The transparent overlay is sensitive to GPU drivers.
  • Lost your save? Saves live in your user data folder. On Windows: %APPDATA%\Godot\app_userdata\Reefling\
  • Want a refund? Refunds are handled by Steam Support.
